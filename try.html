E-Learning as the name implies is a formalised teaching which includes electronic resources. The use of computer & internet is the main component of E-learning. It also reduces our time & effort.
There can be a productive discussion between a teachers& a student irrespective of place & time. E-learning enhances the educational quality. E learning has the facility of chat room so that children can Share their point of view on the topic taught. E-learning is also flexible for teachers because they can set the working hours according to their needs.
